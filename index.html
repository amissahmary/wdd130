Ghana

Ghana is a West African country and the first to gain independence from colonial rule in the year 1957. The country is rich in culture and heritage. It has 16 regions with Accra as the capital. Its neighbouring countries are Ivory Coast, Burkina Faso and Togo. Our official language is English and some of our native languages are Twi, Fante, Ga and Ewe. Popular Ghanaian meals are fufu, ampesi, akpele, kenkey, waakye and tuo zaafi.
